About Us

DataSocle is a software as a service that goes beyond data. Our platform gives you the liberty to not just extract data, but also to transform and push it to third party tools.

Extract

Scrape or Import data from multiple sources across the web or from data files.

Sanitize

Run standard or custom scripts. algorithms or other data transformation routines to enrich your data into information

Customize

Create custom data models in any format like JSON, SQL, CSV, XML etc. to suit your needs.

Reuse

Pull your data models and formats into any 3rd party application or service using our Restful APIs using bulk export.

Features

Data Scole offers data as a service. Our endeavour is to ensure that your data is securely extracted, stored and is reused on an on-demand basis without having other overhead costs involved.

Data Acquisition

You can use DataScole purely as a data gathering tool / scraper, while you handle data processing and storage.

Data Storage

You can choose to store your bulk data or data models with us, securely and indefinitely according to the pricing plan. Your Data is available on a ready to use state at all times with DataSocle.

Data Integration

At Data Scole, 100% uptime of data is our main focus and you can access your data at any time using pull requests and custom session or time based workflows.
